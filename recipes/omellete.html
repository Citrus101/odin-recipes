Omellete

Description

An egg battered and seasoned with veggies cooked in a frying pan

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp onions
  • 1 tbsp capsicum
  • 1 tbsp tomato
  • 1/8 tbsp Salt
  • 1 Green chili
  • 1 Pinch turmeric
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp Coriander leaves

Steps to prepare

  1. Wash and chop onions,tomatoes,capsicum,chilies and coriander leaves finely
  2. Mix together with salt and turmeric
  3. Break the eggs in the bowl and check for shells
  4. Beat the egg until frothy
  5. Add oil to the pan and heat it on medium flame. Do not let the oil become very hot it will make the omellete brown without cooking it well
  6. Pour the egg mixture, allow it to cook while being set on medium flame
  7. When the base firms up, flip it to the other side and cook it till done. Sprinkle crushed pepper and chilli powder if you like
  8. Serve Omellete hot with rice,roti or bread
